01 · THE PROMISE

Every PUSD
is a dollar.
Not a bet.

1PUSD is fully collateralised by real stablecoins — USDC and USDT — held in a single on-chain reserve. No algorithmic component. No rebase. No yield contamination on the base asset.

2Redemption routes in three paths: preferred asset if available; otherwise proportional basket across every reserve; under stress, a forced emergency drain of at-risk positions. The order is the contract; the contract is the promise.

3Fees exist to steer inventory, not to extract rent. Redeem into the abundant asset, pay almost nothing. Redeem into the thin one, pay slightly more. The math is on-chain and the ceiling is 2%.

I.
Collateralised. Plainly.
The reserve contract holds USDC and USDT on the chain they were deposited from. Mint is a deposit; redeem is a withdrawal. The ERC-20 knows nothing about collateral — which is exactly why it can be trusted with your balance.
II.
Multi-chain. Single ledger.
Push Chain's universal accounts let liquidity enter from Ethereum, Solana, Base, Arbitrum or BNB. PUSD is issued on one ledger, redeemable against the whole basket — no wrapping, no bridging tokens in your wallet.
III.
Upgrade-gated. Emergency-aware.
Upgrades pass through a timelocked role. Risky assets are moved to REDEEM-ONLY or EMERGENCY-REDEEM without pausing the protocol. The basket path guarantees exit even when preferred liquidity is thin.
